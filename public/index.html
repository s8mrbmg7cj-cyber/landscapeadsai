Common Questions
Quick Answers
Do I need design experience? +

No. You just fill in your business name, city, service, and offer — then upload a lawn photo. The tool handles all the layout, design, and copywriting. No Canva, no Photoshop, no experience needed.

Can I use my own lawn photos? +

Yes. Upload any photo from your phone or computer and it gets placed directly on the ad canvas. Real photos of your work always outperform stock images — homeowners want to see your actual results.

Can I create ads for Facebook and Instagram? +

Yes. You can download in Square (1080×1080) for Facebook and Instagram Feed, or Portrait (1080×1350) for Stories. Matching captions for Facebook and Instagram are generated alongside every ad.

What does the free plan include? +

The free plan includes 3 ad generations. Each generation can include up to 5 different angle variations, a social caption, and a campaign setup guide. Downloads include a small watermark. Pro removes the watermark and gives you unlimited generations.
